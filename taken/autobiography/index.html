Autobiography

Introduction

My name is Ward Adriaensen, I’m 17 years old and I’m currently studying IT management at Immaculata Institute. The main goal of this essay is to tell you something about my life as an IT’er!

The first contact and beyond

When I was 6, my parents bought us a computer. At that moment we didn’t had a desk to place this PC on so they put it on my study desk in my room. At that age, I already was fascinated by that thing. I had a nice music collection together with my dad and learned to use the PC step by step. My first contact with ‘gaming’ was on the Super Nintendo console. I played games like Mario All Stars, The Blues Brothers, Earthworm Jim … .

Later on, I discovered computer gaming and started playing games like Lego Racers, Age of Empires II, King Kong and KetnetKick. At the age of 8, I started playing Runescape, an MMORPG. It quickly became an addiction; playing a nice game, TOGETHER with friends, IN the same game, ONLINE! At this time I already was fascinated by the ‘art’ of hacking. I had a program, “Cheat Engine”, wich made it possible to change numbers in online games. With this software I changed the amounts of money and became rich in every game. I felt like a real hacker! I also spent many hours of time with jailbreaking iPhones and iPods, getting free in-game money and free games (this was very illegal ofcourse). Later on I played games like Minecraft, League of Legends, Call of Duty, Battlefield … .

In my life, I had a lot of consoles :

  • Super Nintendo (SNES)
  • Gameboy Color
  • Gameboy Advance SP
  • Nintendo DS
  • Playstation Portable (PSP)
  • Playstation 2
  • WII
  • Ipod Touch (I also played a lot of games on this thing :-) )

IT: My life!

IT is an important part of my life. On normal school days I spend about 2-3 hours on my computer. I only game on wednesday and in the weekend. The time I spend on my PC on school days goes to schoolwork etc. When I am gaming, the time flies by very quickly! I think I game about 16 hours a week. Actually I’m connected 24/7 to the internet: smartphone, tablet … . I’m always busy checking mails, surfing, watching videos or streams and texting friends. I’m interested in all kind of things related to the computer e.g. building my own PC, experimenting with things like the Raspberry Pi, fixing computer issues of friends etc. .

My future

After finishing this year, I’m probably going to study computer applications (toegepaste informatica) in Antwerp. In this field of study I will be able to choose between softwaremanagement, networkmanagement or application development. At this moment I haven’t made my choice yet. Working at the Computer Crime unit of the police seems to be an exciting job for me, so I can immerse myself in the hackersworld on a legal way this time :-)
